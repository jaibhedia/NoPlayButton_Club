About Us

Why NoPlayButton?

For students to learn development skills, solve problems through technology and inspire them to be world class developers and changemakers.

Concept of NoPlayButton

The NoPlayButton program is a grassroots channel through which Google provides development, mobile & web development skills for students, towards employability.

Target audience

NoPlayButton activities are targeted at University students and any others including faculty members who want to learn development skills & work to solve real-life problems.

Technologies

Opportunities to learn & access deep technical content.

App Development

Every year google developers release exciting new updates to the world's most popular operating system. We always have sessions to keep you updated and mastering the latest trends in modern android development.

Web Development

Learn the core foundations of a delightful web experience both for the user and developer. Stay up to tabs with emerging and trending technologies. Get access to a guided, tutorial and hands-on coding experience.

Cyber Security

Data is valuable. With so much sensitive information online, companies need cybersecurity pros to protect it. This course is a great starting point for anyone interested in cybersecurity, but not sure where to start. Build your foundation by learning the fundamentals of one of the fastest-growing fields today.

Machine Learning

Learn how to drive user engagement and retention with intelligent apps that are able to effectively serve users what they need without the fuss by providing these systems with the ability to utomatically learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.

Game Development

Dive into the vibrant world of game development, where we blend advanced technology with creative storytelling. Our team focuses on crafting immersive experiences, pushing boundaries with AI-driven characters and dynamic environments. We're passionate about creating games that not only entertain but also transport players into rich, interactive worlds. Join us in shaping the future of gaming, where innovation meets imagination, and every game is an adventure into new realms of possibility. Together, let's redefine gaming, making each experience more engaging, visually stunning, and uniquely captivating for players worldwide.

Artificial Intelligence

Embark on an AI odyssey, where deep learning and advanced algorithms drive breakthroughs across various sectors. Our commitment to AI innovation leads to transformative solutions in healthcare, environmental sustainability, and beyond. Join us in harnessing AI's potential to shape a smarter, more connected world. Together, let's explore AI's limitless possibilities, making significant impacts and unlocking new frontiers.
