About Us

Why NoPlayButton?

For students to learn development skills, solve problems through technology and inspire them to be world class developers and changemakers.

Concept of NoPlayButton

The NoPlayButton program is a grassroots channel through which Google provides development, mobile & web development skills for students, towards employability.

Target audience

NoPlayButton activities are targeted at University students and any others including faculty members who want to learn development skills & work to solve real-life problems.

Technologies

Opportunities to learn & access deep technical content.

App Development

Every year google developers release exciting new updates to the world's most popular operating system. We always have sessions to keep you updated and mastering the latest trends in modern android development.

Web Development

Welcome to the vibrant realm of Web Development, where code and creativity converge to build the digital face of the internet. Our Web Development domain is dedicated to unraveling the art and science of creating websites. From HTML and CSS to advanced JavaScript frameworks, we cover a wide spectrum of technologies and methodologies. This domain is a playground for both aspiring and seasoned developers to learn, experiment, and showcase their skills in building responsive, efficient, and aesthetically pleasing websites. Join us in this journey of continuous learning and innovation, as we create web solutions that are not just functional but also set new standards in user experience.

Cyber Security

Data is valuable. With so much sensitive information online, companies need cybersecurity pros to protect it. This course is a great starting point for anyone interested in cybersecurity, but not sure where to start. Build your foundation by learning the fundamentals of one of the fastest-growing fields today.

Machine Learning

Learn how to drive user engagement and retention with intelligent apps that are able to effectively serve users what they need without the fuss by providing these systems with the ability to utomatically learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.

Game Development

Android Development

Welcome to the dynamic world of Android Development, a realm where creativity meets technology. Our Android Development domain is dedicated to exploring the endless possibilities of mobile applications. Here, we dive into the intricacies of Android OS, mastering languages like Java and Kotlin, and harnessing the power of Android Studio. Our domain provides a platform for enthusiasts to learn, innovate, and contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of mobile technology. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, join us in shaping the future of mobile applications, creating user-friendly, impactful, and cutting-edge apps that redefine the way we interact with our digital world.

Blockchain

Enter the revolutionary domain of Blockchain, a technology that is redefining the digital world. Our Blockchain domain is a hub for exploring the decentralized networks that are transforming industries. We delve into the principles of distributed ledgers, smart contracts, and cryptocurrency, unraveling the complexities of this groundbreaking technology. This domain offers a comprehensive learning journey, from understanding the basics of blockchain to developing secure and transparent solutions. Join us in this exciting venture, where we not only learn about blockchain technology but also contribute to real-world applications that exemplify trust, security, and innovation.
